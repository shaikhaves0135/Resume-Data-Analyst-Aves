Q1. A developer is assigned a task to scrape 1 lakh website pages from a directory site, while scrapping he is facing such hcaptcha, which are placed to stop people from scrapping As a project Coordinator suggest ways to solve this problem ?

ANS: Scraping Websites with hCaptcha:
Solving hCaptcha challenges during web scraping can be tricky, but there are a few ways to handle this issue:

Use Anti-Captcha Services: Consider using anti-captcha services like Anti-Captcha, 2Captcha, or DeathByCaptcha. These services solve captchas for you, and you can integrate their APIs into your scraping script.

Solving Captchas Automatically: Utilize libraries like Python's selenium with WebDriver to automate solving captchas. Be mindful that this method might violate the terms of service of some websites, so proceed with caution.

Rotate IP Addresses: Rotate your IP addresses and use a pool of proxies. This helps in bypassing captcha challenges as websites won't be able to track a single source making numerous requests.
